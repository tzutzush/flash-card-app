You can choose between modes to learn. Read more about them below. To start learning, choose a category.

You will see both sides of the card in front of you. You can cycle through your cards indefinitely. This mode is ideal for memorization of new words.

Noun

Words used to identify people, places, or things

You will see 9 cards (or less if you don't have enough) on one page. When you click on them, the solution is revealed. Then you can choose if you were right or not. On these clicks the solution will always appear, so we make sure you learn the correct meaning.
